# MGSF SEO Content-Gap Report

Source: InfraNodus `analyze_google_search_results` on MGSF's core lane terms
(spray foam insulation montana · closed cell spray foam glendive mt · concrete lifting montana ·
pole barn insulation · metal building insulation · spray foam roofing · crawl space encapsulation montana).
**Research deliverable — no pages auto-edited. Clifton's call on what to build.**

## Big picture
The SERP graph is **"focused" / top-node-heavy** — `insulation` and `foam` dominate everything
(betweenness 0.80 and 0.31). Translation: competing head-on for "spray foam insulation" is a
saturated brawl. The winnable ground is the **under-covered clusters** where intent is specific
and competition is thinner. Those are below, ranked by opportunity.

## Content gaps InfraNodus surfaced (competitors bridge these; we're thin)
1. **Foam Roofing → Crawl Space** — vapor barrier · encapsulation · radiant · crawlspace moisture.
2. **Insulation (pole barn / metal) → Concrete Repair** — slab repair · lifting · "free (estimate)".
3. **Concrete Repair → Energy Efficiency** — moisture · condensation · thermal (nobody ties slab/void
   work to the moisture story; we can).

## Prioritized recommendations (mapped to our 19 pages)

### P1 — Build a dedicated Crawl Space Encapsulation page (MISSING)
"Crawl space encapsulation" is its own search cluster (~13–17% of results) and we have **no page** for
it — only a mention inside the service architecture. Own terms: encapsulation, vapor barrier, ground
cover, radiant barrier, rim-joist air sealing, crawlspace moisture/condensation. This is the single
biggest gap: real regional intent, no MGSF page. Cross-links naturally to spray-foam-insulation +
concrete + soil work.

### P2 — Add "free estimate" intent language site-wide
`free` shows up as a **conceptual gateway** in the concrete/insulation clusters — competitors rank on
"free estimate / free quote." Confirm every service + city page has an explicit "Free Estimate" CTA in
copy + a heading/alt that search can read (not just a button). Cheap, high-leverage.

### P3 — Open-cell vs closed-cell education page/section
`cell open closed` and even `reddit` cluster under Foam Roofing — people are searching the open-vs-closed
debate and reputation/Q&A content. An honest comparison page (we run closed-cell; say why, per DOCTRINE)
captures informational traffic and feeds the FAQPage schema we already have.

### P4 — Bridge concrete/slab work to the moisture story
Gap 3: no one connects concrete lifting / void fill to condensation/thermal/moisture. Our concrete +
(new) crawl-space pages can cross-link on "water is one system" — slab-edge water, crawl moisture, void
washout — differentiating us as building-performance, not just a lifting service. (Mirrors the new
GAP_BRIDGES block just added to Klyfton's brain.)

### P5 — Radiant barrier coverage
`radiant` appears in the Crawl Space cluster and we don't address it. A short section (radiant barrier vs
spray foam, when each applies) closes the loop for attic/crawl searchers.

## Out of scope (noted, not recommended)
- **Kalispell / western Montana** rank in the concrete cluster but are **outside our lane** (we run
  eastern MT + ND/SD/WY). Don't chase Kalispell terms.
- **North/South Dakota** cluster is small in this sample but IS our lane — our ND/SD city pages already
  cover it; keep them.

## Suggested build order
1. Crawl Space Encapsulation page (P1) — highest intent, zero current coverage.
2. Free-estimate language sweep (P2) — fast, site-wide.
3. Open vs closed-cell page (P3) + radiant section (P5) — informational capture.
4. Concrete↔moisture cross-linking (P4) — ties the brand story together.
